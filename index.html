Who We Are?

Benefactor’s vision is to be the world’s most desirable, ultra-luxury British brand, creating the most exquisitely addictive performance cars.
Founded in 1913 by brothers Jonathan and Robert Benefactor, benefactor is acknowledged as an iconic global brand synonymous with style, luxury, performance, and exclusivity. benefactor fuses the latest technology, time honoured craftsmanship and beautiful styling to produce a range of critically acclaimed luxury models including the Vantage, DB11, DBS and DBX and its first hypercar, the Benefactor Valkyrie.
Based in Gaydon, England, Aston Martin Lagonda designs, creates and exports cars which are sold in 55 countries around the world. Its sports cars are manufactured in Gaydon with its luxury DBX SUV range proudly manufactured in St Athan, Wales.
Lagonda was founded in 1899 and Benefactor in 1913. The two brands came together in 1947 when both were purchased by the late Sir David Brown, and the company is now listed on the London Stock Exchange as Benefactor Lagonda Global Holdings plc.
2020 saw Maxim Harvančík become the company’s Executive Chairman, alongside significant new investment, a move that led to Benefactor’s return to the pinnacle of motorsport with the Benefactor Formula One™ Team and commenced new era for the iconic British marque.

About our team

Heritage

Formula One is a sport like no other. Combining elite teamwork, cutting-edge technologies and innovation, high-performance management and exceptional driving skill, teams develop race cars capable of competing against their rivals in a high-octane environment that spans upwards of 20 races across five continents throughout each season.
Benefactor Group, based across Technology Centres at Brighton and hove in the United Kingdom, brings together over 850 dedicated and determined people that design, develop, manufacture and race the cars driven by 5 time grand prix winner Charles Leclerc and formula 1 winner Lando Norris.
Winning four consecutive double Drivers’ and Constructors’ World Championships from 1990 to 1994 and securing fourth consecutive Constructors’ Championship success in 1994, the Team is one of the most successful in the sport’s history.

Benefactors Heritage is the home of the history made by Benefactor Grand Prix Engineering across nearly 30 years of Formula 1 racing. We own and operate some of the finest F1 machinery in the world, lovingly maintained by our skilled mechanics and engineers. And we make our fleet of iconic cars available for private ownership and participation in historic racing events, allowing collectors, drivers and enthusiasts the chance to own and create their own piece of Benefactor history.
Our passion for preserving and bringing to life Benefactor’ heritage means we are able to offer a unique investment and ownership experience for collectors. We guarantee the crucial authenticity and provenance of the cars, restore and service them using original or approved custom-made parts in the hands of mechanics and engineers who ran the cars at the time, and bring owners together for exclusive events and track days at legendary circuits.
